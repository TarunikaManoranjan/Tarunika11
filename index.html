  • Some could pace two heart chambers instead of just one.
  • They became smaller and more reliable.

Modern Pacemakers (1990s‚ÄìToday)

  • Now pacemakers are very small and can be monitored remotely.
  • Some are even MRI-safe.
  • New types like leadless pacemakers don‚Äôt need wires at all.

When it‚Äôs used: For people whose heart beats too slowly, and only one chamber needs help.

Why it's good:

  • Doesn‚Äôt work as well for people who need both chambers paced

  • No scars
